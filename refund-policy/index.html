1. General Provisions

This Refund Policy defines the conditions under which clients may request a refund for AI-based moderation services for Telegram chats.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be granted in the following cases:

  • Technical payment error (e.g., double charge)
  • Inability to activate the Service due to the fault of the Service Provider
  • Cancellation of the subscription within 14 calendar days from activation, provided that no more than 10% of the message processing limit has been used

3. Refund Procedure

  • The Client contacts customer support via the Telegram bot or email
  • Provides the reason for the refund request and their account ID
  • The support team reviews the request within 3 (three) business days
  • If approved, the refund is issued to the Client’s bank card within 5–10 business days

4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are NOT provided in the following cases:

  • After successful activation of the subscription and usage of more than 10% of the message processing limit

In exceptional cases, the Service Provider may, at its sole discretion, offer a partial refund or extend the Service period based on the specific circumstances.
